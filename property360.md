PROPERTY 360 — SHOW PROFILE
Property 360 is a premium multimedia real estate platform dedicated to educating, informing, and shaping conversations around property ownership, development, investment, and management in Uganda and the wider East African region.
Originally developed as a flagship television program airing on NBS Television, Property 360 extends far beyond traditional broadcast. The show is designed as a multi-platform media experience, with content distributed across television, digital streaming, social media, and on-demand platforms. This approach ensures that property knowledge, market insights, and industry conversations reach audiences wherever they consume content—on TV, online, and on mobile.
Property 360 explores the entire ecosystem of the built environment, offering viewers and digital audiences a comprehensive understanding of real estate—from land acquisition and development to architecture, construction, financing, property management, and investment strategy. Through expert interviews, site visits, project showcases, and thought-leadership discussions, the show highlights the professionals, institutions, and innovations shaping Uganda’s evolving property landscape.
The platform was created to address a critical gap in the market. While real estate remains one of the most important drivers of wealth creation and urban development, many investors, homeowners, and developers operate with limited access to reliable industry information. Property 360 bridges this gap by translating complex real estate concepts into practical, accessible insights for both industry professionals and the general public.
Each episode features industry experts, developers, regulators, architects, financiers, and property managers, offering credible perspectives on the opportunities and challenges shaping the market. Through this ecosystem-driven approach, the show provides viewers with practical knowledge on topics such as property investment, urban development, construction technologies, housing finance, regulatory frameworks, facilities management, and sustainable development.
As a multimedia platform, Property 360 extends the conversation beyond broadcast through digital clips, extended interviews, social media discussions, and online replays, allowing audiences to engage with the content in deeper and more interactive ways. This multiplatform strategy not only broadens audience reach but also creates ongoing dialogue around the future of real estate in Uganda.
Property 360 is hosted and executive produced by Nsamba Wilberforce, Chief Executive Officer of Credo Management Limited. Wilberforce is a Certified International Property Specialist (CIPS) and Accredited Buyer’s Representative (ABR) with National Association of Realtors credentials. With over a decade of real estate practice across Uganda, East Africa, and international markets, he brings global standards and local market expertise to the platform.
Through credible storytelling, expert insight, and national media reach, Property 360 positions itself as a trusted hub for real estate intelligence, industry leadership, and informed property conversations.
Ultimately, the platform seeks to contribute to a more transparent, professional, and informed real estate sector, empowering individuals and institutions to make smarter property decisions while supporting the growth and modernisation of the built environment in Uganda.
